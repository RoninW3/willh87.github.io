About me

Hey there! Thanks for visiting my portfolio. My name is William Hicks and I currently reside in Las Vegas, NV.

I am currently a student with MIT xPRO studying web development with emphasis on MERN. On this website you will find current and past projects with MIT xPRO, as well as independent projects I am always working on.

My past work history includes over a decade in clinical healthcare with an emphasis on Emergency Medicine, Critical Care, and Aviation. I have an in-depth knowledge of what works and doesn't work for healthcare providers interacting with software currently in the industry.

My hobbies outside of work include Blacksmithing, riding motorcycles, and reading.
